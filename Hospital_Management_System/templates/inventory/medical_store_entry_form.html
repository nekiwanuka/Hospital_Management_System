Quick Reference Guide
  1. Step 1 Item Search — Start typing to search existing items, or scan a barcode. If the item doesn't exist, click "Add New Item" or press N.
  2. Step 1 Item Type — Medicine for drugs, Supply for reagents/consumables, Device for equipment. Medicine-specific fields appear only for medicines.
  3. Step 2 Quantity & Packaging — Enter how many packs you received, then describe the nesting: Box → Packet → Strip → Tablet. The system auto-computes the grand total.
  4. Step 3 Batch Number — Copy the batch/lot number exactly from the label. Used for FEFO tracking and recall tracing.
  5. Step 3 Expiry Date — Must be a future date. Items approaching expiry will appear in dashboard alerts.
  6. Step 4 Purchase Price — Price paid for ONE purchase pack. The live preview shows cost per unit, selling price, and profit.
  7. Step 4 Price Factor — Multiplication factor (e.g. 1.25 = 25% markup). Selling = unit cost × factor.
  8. Step 4 Manual Override — Click to set a custom selling price instead of auto-calculating.
  9. Step 5 Supplier & Location — Select or add the supplier, and choose a storage location.
  10. Security Price Visibility — Purchase cost and margins are visible only on this screen and in Finance reports.
