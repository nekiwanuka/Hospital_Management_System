Patient Journey

Seamless End-to-End Workflow

Every step of the patient journey — digitised, tracked, and billed automatically.

1

Patient Registration

Quick registration with demographics, medical history, allergies, and insurance details. Returning patients are found instantly.

2

Triage Assessment

Nurse captures vitals, symptoms, and assigns a 5-level priority score. Critical cases are flagged immediately.

3

Doctor Consultation

Doctor reviews triage data, examines patient, records diagnosis and treatment plan. Orders labs, radiology, or prescriptions in one click.

4

Investigations & Treatment

Lab collects samples and enters results. Radiology performs scans. Pharmacy dispenses medications. All results flow back to the doctor.

5

Billing & Payment

Every service generates a line item automatically. Cashier processes payment via cash, mobile money, card, bank transfer, or insurance.

6

Discharge & Follow-up

Patient receives printed receipt, discharge summary. Follow-up visits are scheduled. All data is archived for future reference.
